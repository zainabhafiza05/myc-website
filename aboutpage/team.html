Meet The MYC Team

Safa Aynoor

Youth Director

A medical student at the University of Washington School of Medicine, Safa studied Neuroscience and Human Rights on the pre-medical track. With over seven years of experience in youth development and community initiatives, her work spans summer programs, college organizations, youth groups, and refugee empowerment efforts. She is passionate about pioneering intentional spaces that lead with ihsan and faith to bring resources, mentorship, and opportunities for growth to Muslim communities, particularly for Muslimah girls.

Ayesha Patel

Secretary

A Software Engineer at Microsoft and a Computer Science graduate from the University of Washington. She is currently in her second year of the Alimah program and has spent several years working closely with youth in the Eastside through mentorship, teaching, and community programming. Ayesha is passionate about creating welcoming, intentional spaces where young people feel supported and empowered to grow.

Zainab Ateeque

Treasurer & Tech Lead

A Computer Science senior at the University of Washington and a Hafiza in her final year of Islamic Seminary. With over five years of experience organizing, teaching, and volunteering, Zainab is dedicated to building community and making Islamic knowledge accessible and inspiring.
